Overview

Purpose

The Odesa Chamber of Commerce helps local businesses grow and advocates for them. It connects businesses, organizes learning events, and supports businesses in their growth.
Advocacy efforts are described as support and representation of the local businesses, the work is to influence decisions within political, economic, and social systems and institutions that directly affect businesses in Odesa.

Scenarios

  1. Business Support Inquiry: “I'm new to business in Odesa. How can the Chamber help me in terms of resources and assistance?”
  2. Membership Benefits: “I consider joining the Chamber. How will my business benefit from the membership?”
  3. Advocacy Efforts: “How does the Chamber ensures that interests of local businesses are protected? Is there any advocacy initiatives currently?”
